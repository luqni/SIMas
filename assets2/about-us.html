Why I Started This Website

السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ


(As-Salam Alaykoum - Peace Be Upon You)

My name is Nazim Ali and I decided to start this website to help beautify Islamic websites and provide resources to help anyone that needs help with their Islamic Center, Masjid, or Islamic events.

I noticed that a lot of Islamic websites on the internet have terrible design, are not compatible with mobile devices, and use layouts/designs from the 90's.

I hope that this helps the Muslim community :)
